## Understanding Your Role

### C&P Staff Roles

| Role | What You Can Do |
|------|-----------------|
| **VRM** (Vendor Relations Manager) | Process Stage A→B and Stage D→E |
| **HOD** (Head of Department) | Process Stage B→C, tag end-users, Stage E→F approvals |
| **Supervisor** | Process Stage B→C, tag end-users |
| **CO** (Contracts Officer) | Process Stage D→E |
| **Executive Approver** | Final approval Stage F→L3 |
| **Admin** | Full access to all stages |
| **C&P Staff** | General C&P staff access |
| **User** (Amni Staff) | End-user reviews at Stage C→D |

### Approval Stage Permissions

Your role determines which stages you can approve:

**Stage A to B**:
- VRM processes initial document review

**Stage B to C**:
- HOD or Supervisor approves
- Must tag an Amni Staff end-user who heads the department the contractor belongs to

**Stage C to D**:
- The tagged end-user (Amni Staff) carries out the review

**Stage D to E**:
- VRM or CO processes

**Stage E to F**:
- HOD carries out (right before executive approval)

**Stage F to L3**:
- Executive Approver carries out final approval

## Inviting Contractors

### How to Send an Invitation

1. Click **"Registration Invites"** in the sidebar
2. Click **"Create New Invite"** or **"Send Invitation"**
3. Fill in the contractor details:
   ```
   Company Name: ABC Construction Ltd
   Contact Person: John Smith
   Email: john.smith@abcconstruction.com
   Phone: +234-xxx-xxx-xxxx
   Recommended By: Your Name/Department
   ```
4. Click **"Send Invitation"**
5. Contractor receives email with unique registration link

### Managing Invitations

View invitations in tabs:
- **Active**: Valid invitations waiting for contractor to register
- **Expired**: Invitations past expiry date
- **Used**: Contractor completed registration
- **Archived**: Invitations no longer needed

### Renewing Expired Invitations

If an invitation expires before the contractor registers:

1. Go to **"Expired"** tab
2. Find the invitation
3. Click **"Renew"** button
4. System sends new invitation email with extended expiry date

### Sending Reminder Emails

For active invitations where contractor hasn't responded:

1. Find the invitation in **"Active"** tab
2. Click **"Send Reminder"** button
3. Contractor receives follow-up email

### Archiving Invitations

If an invitation is no longer needed:

1. Find the invitation
2. Click **"Archive"** button
3. Confirm action
4. Invitation moves to "Archived" tab

## Registration Approvals

### Accessing Applications

1. Click **"Registration Approvals"** in the sidebar
2. You'll see tabs for different application statuses

### Understanding the Tabs

| Tab | What's Here | Your Action |
|-----|-------------|-------------|
| **Pending L2** | New applications at various stages awaiting review | Review and approve/return based on your role |
| **Completed L2** | Applications that passed initial stages | Review if your stage |
| **Returned to Contractor** | Applications sent back for revisions | Wait for contractor to resubmit |
| **In Progress** | Currently being reviewed | Continue if you're reviewing |
| **L3** | Executive-level approvals | Executive Approver only |
| **Invited Contractors** | Track contractor invitation status | Monitor invited contractors |
| **Park Requests** | Applications on hold | Approve or decline hold requests |

### Viewing Application Details

1. Navigate to appropriate tab (usually **"Pending L2"**)
2. Find the application:
   - Scroll through the list
   - Or use search box (company name, email)
3. Click on the application row to open full details
4. Review all sections:
   - Company information
   - Uploaded documents
   - Certificates and expiry dates
   - Services offered
   - Financial information
   - References

## Approval Workflow & Stages

### The 7-Stage Process

Applications move through stages A → B → C → D → E → F → L3

```
Stage A: Initial review and verification
   ↓ (VRM processes)
Stage B: Department assignment and categorization
   ↓ (HOD/Supervisor processes + tags end-user)
Stage C: End-user departmental review
   ↓ (Tagged Amni Staff end-user reviews)
Stage D: Post-review verification
   ↓ (VRM or CO processes)
Stage E: Final checks before executive
   ↓ (HOD processes)
Stage F: Executive review
   ↓ (Executive Approver processes)
L3: Approved and active
```

### Stage A → B (VRM Only)

**Purpose**: Initial document review and verification

**Your Tasks**:
1. Verify all required documents uploaded
2. Check document quality (clear, complete, legible)
3. Review company information for completeness
4. Approve to move to Stage B

**How to Approve**:
1. Review all sections carefully
2. Click **"Approve to Stage B"** button
3. Application moves to Stage B

### Stage B → C (HOD or Supervisor)

**Purpose**: Assign department and tag responsible end-user

**Your Tasks**:
1. Review company details and services
2. Determine which AMNI department this contractor belongs to
3. **Assign job categories** (if applicable)
4. **Tag an Amni Staff end-user** who heads that department
5. Approve to move to Stage C

**How to Tag End-Users**:
1. In the approval view, find **"Assign End-Users"** section
2. Click to select end-users
3. Choose the Amni Staff member who heads the relevant department
4. Save selection
5. Click **"Approve to Stage C"** button

**Important**: You MUST tag an end-user before approving to Stage C. This person will review the application at Stage C.

### Stage C → D (Tagged Amni Staff End-User)

**Purpose**: Department-specific review by the tagged end-user

**If You're the Tagged End-User**:
1. You'll see the application in your **"Pending L2"** tab
2. Review documents specific to your department
3. Verify contractor meets departmental requirements
4. Either approve or return with feedback
5. Click **"Approve to Stage D"** button when satisfied

### Stage D → E (VRM or CO)

**Purpose**: Post end-user review verification

**Your Tasks**:
1. Review end-user feedback (if any)
2. Verify all previous stage requirements met
3. Conduct final verification checks
4. Approve to move to Stage E

**How to Approve**:
1. Review all sections and previous stage comments
2. Click **"Approve to Stage E"** button
3. Application moves to Stage E

### Stage E → F (HOD)

**Purpose**: Final checks before executive approval

**Your Tasks**:
1. Conduct final comprehensive review
2. Verify all stages completed satisfactorily
3. Check no outstanding issues
4. Approve to move to Stage F (executive level)

**How to Approve**:
1. Complete thorough final review
2. Click **"Approve to Stage F"** button
3. Application moves to executive approval stage

### Stage F → L3 (Executive Approver)

**Purpose**: Final executive approval

**Your Tasks**:
1. Executive-level business review
2. Final decision on contractor approval
3. Approve to activate contractor

**How to Approve**:
1. Review complete application history
2. Click **"Approve to L3"** button
3. Contractor becomes fully approved and active

## Daily Tasks

### Morning Routine

1. **Log in** to staff portal

1. **Work through applications systematically**

4. **Respond to queries** from contractors or colleagues

1. **Complete in-progress reviews**
2. **Check for new arrivals**
3. **Note any follow-ups** needed tomorrow
4. **Log out**

## Common Actions

### Approving an Application

**When Application is Complete and Meets Requirements**:

1. Open the application
2. Review ALL sections carefully:
   - Company registration details
   - Uploaded documents (licenses, insurance, etc.)
   - Certificate expiry dates (must not be expired)
   - Financial information
   - References
3. Verify documents are clear and legible
4. Check all required fields completed
5. Add any remarks/comments for next stage (optional but helpful)
6. Click **"Approve to Stage [X]"** button
7. Application advances to next stage

**Important**: The system prevents double-clicking to avoid duplicate actions.

### Returning an Application

**When Information is Missing, Incorrect, or Unclear**:

1. Open the application
2. Identify which sections need attention
3. Click on each section that needs changes
4. Add **specific, detailed remarks**:
   - ✅ Good: "Business license image is blurry. Please upload a clearer scan showing the license number and expiry date."
   - ❌ Bad: "Documents unclear"
5. List ALL issues at once to avoid multiple revision cycles
6. Click **"Return to Contractor"** button
7. Contractor receives notification with your feedback
8. Application moves to **"Returned to Contractor"** tab

**Best Practice**: Be as specific as possible so contractor knows exactly what to fix.

### Parking an Application

**When You Need More Time or Information Before Deciding**:

1. Open the application
2. Click **"Park Request"** button
3. Add reason for parking:
   ```
   Example: "Waiting for legal department review of contract terms"
   Example: "Need clarification on insurance coverage from risk team"
   ```
4. Click confirm
5. Application moves to **"Park Requests"** tab

### Approving Park Requests

**When You're Ready to Continue a Parked Application**:

1. Go to **"Park Requests"** tab
2. Find the parked application
3. Verify the issue is resolved
4. Click **"Approve Park Request"** button
5. Application returns to normal workflow

### Declining Park Requests

**If Application Should Not Be Parked**:

1. Go to **"Park Requests"** tab
2. Find the application
3. Click **"Decline Park Request"** button
4. Application returns to previous stage

### Using Search

**To Find a Specific Application**:

1. Locate search box at top of page
2. Enter search term:
   - Company name: "ABC Construction"
   - Contact email: "john@company.com"
   - Application ID: "VEN123456"
3. Results appear in real-time

### Exporting Data

**To Export Applications to Excel**:

1. Navigate to the tab you want to export (Pending L2, Completed L2, etc.)
2. Apply any filters if needed
3. Click **"Export to Excel"** button
4. File downloads automatically
5. Open in Excel for reporting/analysis

**Use Cases**:
- Monthly approval reports
- Tracking approval metrics
- Sharing with management
- Auditing

## Common Scenarios

### Scenario 1: Application Missing Documents

**Situation**: Contractor submitted application but business license not uploaded

**What to Do**:
1. Open application
2. Navigate to section where business license should be
3. Add remark: "Business registration license document not uploaded. Please upload PDF or clear scan of your business license."
4. Check for any other missing items
5. Click **"Return to Contractor"**
6. Wait for contractor to resubmit

### Scenario 2: Expired Certificate

**Situation**: Insurance certificate expired 2 months ago

**What to Do**:
1. Do NOT approve application with expired certificate
2. Add remark: "Insurance certificate expired on [date]. Please upload current, valid insurance certificate."
3. Click **"Return to Contractor"**
4. Contractor must provide updated certificate

### Scenario 3: Document is Blurry/Unreadable

**Situation**: Uploaded scan is too blurry to read

**What to Do**:
1. Add remark: "[Document name] image is too blurry to read. Please upload a clearer scan or photo showing all details including license number, dates, and company name."
2. Click **"Return to Contractor"**

### Scenario 4: Assigning End-User at Stage B

**Situation**: You're HOD processing Stage B → C, need to tag department head

**What to Do**:
1. Review contractor's services/category
2. Determine relevant AMNI department (e.g., Engineering, Procurement, Facilities)
3. In application, find **"Assign End-Users"** section
4. Click to open end-user selection
5. Search for and select the Amni Staff member who heads that department
6. Confirm selection
7. Add remark noting which department assigned: "Assigned to Engineering department for technical review"
8. Click **"Approve to Stage C"**
9. Tagged end-user will see application in their Pending L2

### Scenario 5: End-User Review at Stage C

**Situation**: You're the tagged end-user for Stage C review

**What to Do**:
1. Check **"Pending L2"** tab for applications assigned to you
2. Open application
3. Review documents relevant to your department
4. Verify contractor meets your department's requirements
5. Check technical qualifications, certifications, experience
6. If satisfied: Add remarks with your findings, click **"Approve to Stage D"**
7. If issues: Add specific remarks on what's needed, click **"Return to Contractor"**

### Scenario 6: Application Looks Good

**Situation**: All documents present, valid, clear, and accurate

**What to Do**:
1. Complete thorough review
2. Verify certificate expiry dates are valid
3. Check all sections filled completely
4. Add remarks for next stage reviewer (optional):
   - "All documents verified and in order"
   - "Contractor has strong references and relevant experience"
5. Click **"Approve to Stage [X]"** button
6. Application advances

## Best Practices

### For Efficient Reviews

**Do**:
- ✅ Review applications in order received (FIFO)
- ✅ Check ALL sections before approving
- ✅ Verify certificate expiry dates
- ✅ Add clear, specific feedback when returning
- ✅ Use remarks to communicate with next stage reviewer
- ✅ Tag appropriate end-users at Stage B
- ✅ Document reasons when parking applications

**Don't**:
- ❌ Rush through reviews
- ❌ Approve applications with expired certificates
- ❌ Give vague feedback ("needs work", "incomplete")
- ❌ Skip sections during review
- ❌ Forget to tag end-users at Stage B
- ❌ Leave parked applications indefinitely

### Communication Tips

When returning applications:

1. **Be Specific**:
   - Instead of: "Documents missing"
   - Say: "Please upload: 1) Business license, 2) Tax certificate, 3) Insurance certificate"

2. **Be Helpful**:
   - Explain what you need and why
   - "Upload contractor safety certificate showing current safety training for all field staff"

3. **Be Complete**:
   - List ALL issues in one return
   - Avoid multiple back-and-forth cycles
   - Check every section before returning

4. **Be Professional**:
   - Maintain courteous, helpful tone
   - Focus on requirements, not criticism

### Certificate Verification

Always check:
- ✅ Certificate document uploaded
- ✅ Document is clear and legible
- ✅ Expiry date entered correctly
- ✅ Certificate is still valid (not expired)
- ✅ Certificate type matches requirement

**Red Flags**:
- ⚠️ Certificate expired (do not approve)
- ⚠️ Certificate expiring within 30 days (note in remarks)
- ⚠️ Image too blurry to verify details (return for clearer upload)
- ⚠️ Certificate doesn't match contractor name (investigate)

## Troubleshooting

### Can't See Pending Applications

**Problem**: "Pending L2" tab is empty but you expect applications

**Solutions**:
1. Verify you're on correct tab
2. Check if search filters are applied (clear them)
3. Refresh the page (F5 or reload button)
4. Applications may be at different stage than your role
5. Check with supervisor if issue persists

### Action Button Not Working

**Problem**: "Approve" or "Return" button is grayed out or doesn't respond

**Solutions**:
1. You may not have permission for that stage
2. Application may be at different stage
3. Another user may currently be reviewing it
4. Try refreshing the page
5. Ensure you've added remarks if required
6. Clear browser cache and try again

### Application Disappeared

**Problem**: Application you were reviewing is gone

**Solutions**:
1. Check other tabs - it may have moved
2. Another user may have acted on it
3. Use search to locate it by company name
4. Check Events log (if available) to see what happened
5. Ask colleagues if they processed it

### Can't Tag End-Users at Stage B

**Problem**: End-user selection not working

**Solutions**:
1. Verify you're at Stage B
2. Confirm you have HOD or Supervisor role
3. Check that end-users exist in system
4. Try refreshing the page
5. Contact admin if issue persists

### Forgot to Tag End-User

**Problem**: Approved Stage B without tagging end-user

**Solutions**:
1. Contact HOD or Admin immediately
2. They can use "Revert to L2" to send back to Stage B
3. Re-process and properly tag end-user
4. Application continues correctly

## Appendix: Role Definitions

**VRM** - Vendor Relations Manager: Manages initial contractor verification (A→B) and post-review checks (D→E)

**HOD** - Head of Department: Oversees departmental assignments (B→C), tags end-users, and conducts pre-executive review (E→F)

**Supervisor** - Supervisory staff: Assists with departmental assignments (B→C) and end-user tagging

**CO** - Contracts Officer: Handles post-review verification (D→E)

**Executive Approver** - Executive level: Final approval authority (F→L3)

**Amni Staff** - Department heads: End-users tagged for departmental review (C→D)

**Admin** - System administrator: Full access to all stages and system functions
