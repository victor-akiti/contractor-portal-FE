## Understanding Your Role

### C&P Staff Roles

| Role | What You Can Do |
|------|-----------------|
| **VRM** (Vendor Relations Manager) | Process Stage A→B and Stage D→E |
| **HOD** (Head of Department) | Process Stage B→C, tag end-users, Stage E→F approvals |
| **Supervisor** | Process Stage B→C, tag end-users |
| **CO** (Contracts Officer) | Process Stage D→E |
| **Executive Approver** | Final approval Stage F→L3 |
| **Admin** | Full access to all stages |
| **C&P Staff** | General C&P staff access |
| **User** (Amni Staff) | End-user reviews at Stage C→D |

### Approval Stage Permissions

Your role determines which stages you can approve:

**Stage A to B**:
- VRM processes initial document review

**Stage B to C**:
- HOD or Supervisor approves
- Must tag an Amni Staff end-user who heads the department the contractor belongs to

**Stage C to D**:
- The tagged end-user (Amni Staff) carries out the review

**Stage D to E**:
- VRM or CO processes

**Stage E to F**:
- HOD carries out (right before executive approval)

**Stage F to L3**:
- Executive Approver carries out final approval

## Inviting Contractors

### How to Send an Invitation

1. Click **"Registration Invites"** in the sidebar
2. Click **"Create New Invite"** or **"Send Invitation"**
3. Fill in the contractor details
4. Click **"Send Invite Link"**
5. Contractor receives email with unique registration link

### Managing Invitations
Under **Registration Approvals** - 
View invitations in tabs:
- **Active**: Valid invitations waiting for contractor to register
- **Expired**: Invitations past expiry date
- **Used**: Contractor completed registration
- **Archived**: Invitations no longer needed

### Renewing Expired Invitations

If an invitation expires before the contractor registers:

1. Go to **"Expired"** tab
2. Find the invitation
3. Click **"Extend Expiry Date"** button
4. System sends new invitation email with extended expiry date

### Sending Reminder Emails

For active invitations where contractor hasn't responded:

1. Find the invitation in **"Active"** tab
2. Click **"Send Reminder"** button
3. Contractor receives follow-up email

### Archiving Invitations

If an invitation is no longer needed:

1. Find the invitation
2. Click **"Archive"** button
3. Confirm action
4. Invitation moves to "Archived" tab

## Registration Approvals

### Accessing Applications

1. Click **"Registration Approvals"** in the sidebar
2. You'll see tabs for different application statuses

### Understanding the Tabs

| Tab | What's Here | Your Action |
|-----|-------------|-------------|
| **Pending L2** | New applications at various stages awaiting review | Review and approve/return based on your role |
| **Completed L2** | Applications that failed at any of stages A to E | Restore to Approval pipeline based on your role |
| **Returned to Contractor** | Applications sent back for revisions | Wait for contractor to resubmit |
| **In Progress** | Currently being updated by contractor | See Updates |
| **L3** | All Approved Contractors | N/A |
| **Invited Contractors** | Track contractor invitation status | Monitor invited contractors |
| **Park Requests** | Applications on hold | Approve or decline hold requests depending on your role |

### Viewing Application Details

1. Navigate to appropriate tab (usually **"Pending L2"**)
2. Find the application:
   - Scroll through the list
   - Or use search box (company name, email)
3. Click on the application name to open full details in read-only view mode
4. Click on "PROCESS STAGE -" to open approval view if you have the appropriate role
5. Review all sections:
   - Company information
   - Uploaded documents
   - Certificates and expiry dates
   - Services offered
   - Financial information
   - Other details

## Approval Workflow & Stages

### The 7-Stage Process

Applications move through stages A → B → C → D → E → F → L3

```
Stage A: Initial review and verification
   ↓ (VRM processes)
Stage B: Department assignment and categorization
   ↓ (HOD/Supervisor processes + tags end-user)
Stage C: End-user departmental review
   ↓ (Tagged Amni Staff end-user reviews)
Stage D: Post-review Due Diligence checks
   ↓ (VRM or CO processes)
Stage E: Final checks before executive
   ↓ (HOD processes)
Stage F: Executive review
   ↓ (Executive Approver processes)
L3: Approved and active
```

### Stage A → B (VRM Only)

**Purpose**: Initial document review and verification

**Your Tasks**:
1. Verify all required documents uploaded
2. Check document quality (clear, complete, legible)
3. Review company information for completeness
4. Approve to move to Stage B or Return to Contractor

**How to Approve**:
1. Review all sections carefully and either mark the Checkbox or leave an internal comment and/or leave a comment for the vendor if information update is required
2. Click **"Finish"** button then follow the approval action instructions beneath
3. Application moves to Stage B or is Returned to Contractor for update

### Stage B → C (HOD or Supervisor)

**Purpose**: Assign department and tag responsible end-user

**Your Tasks**:
1. Review company details and services
2. Determine which AMNI department this contractor is concerned with
3. **Assign job categories** (if applicable)
4. **Tag an Amni Staff end-user** who can approve for that department
5. Approve to move to Stage C

**How to Tag End-Users**:
1. In the approval view, find **"Assign End-Users"** section
2. Click to select end-users
3. Choose the Amni Staff member who can approve for the relevant department
4. Click **"Confirm End Users"** button

**Important**: You MUST tag an end-user before approving to Stage C. This person will review the application at Stage C.

### Stage C → D (Tagged Amni Staff End-User)

**Purpose**: Department-specific review by the tagged end-user

**If You're the Tagged End-User**:
1. You'll see the application highlighted in your **"Pending L2"** tab (If applicable)
2. Verify contractor meets departmental requirements
3. Either approve or request application is parked
4. To Approve select Option 1 (Progress Registration) ->
   a. Select service that contractor should render to your department
   b. Check the box if site visitation is necessary for the contractor
6. Click **"Confirm Your Approval"** button when satisfied

### Stage D → E (VRM or CO)

**Purpose**: Post end-user review Due diligence check

**Your Tasks**:
1. Verify all previous stage requirements met
2. Conduct final verification checks with internet checks and upload checks documents
3. Approve to move to Stage E if the contractor checks out

**How to Approve**:
1. Review all sections
2. Upload documents from internet searches and checks
3. Click **"Approve to Stage E"** button
4. Application moves to Stage E

### Stage E → F (HOD)

**Purpose**: Final checks before executive approval

**Your Tasks**:
1. Conduct final comprehensive review and Due diligence checks
2. Verify all stages completed satisfactorily
3. Check no outstanding issues
4. Approve to move to Stage F (executive level)

**How to Approve**:
1. Complete thorough final review
2. Click **"Approve to Stage F"** button
3. Application moves to executive approval stage

### Stage F → L3 (Executive Approver)

**Purpose**: Final executive approval

**Your Tasks**:
1. Executive-level business review
2. Final decision on contractor approval
3. Approve to activate contractor

**How to Approve**:
1. Review complete application
2. Select the appropriate approval option from the dropdown and the top
3. Contractor becomes fully approved and is moved to L3

## Quick Reference

### Application Status Meanings

| Status | Meaning | What It Means for You |
|--------|---------|----------------------|
| **Stage A** | Initial review | VRM reviews and approves to B |
| **Stage B** | Department assignment | HOD/Supervisor tags end-user, approves to C |
| **Stage C** | End-user review | Tagged Amni Staff reviews, approves to D |
| **Stage D** | Post-review check | VRM/CO verifies, approves to E |
| **Stage E** | Pre-executive review | HOD final check, approves to F |
| **Stage F** | Executive approval | Executive Approver reviews, approves to L3 |
| **L3** | Fully approved | Contractor is onboard |
| **Returned** | Sent back | Waiting for contractor to fix issues |
| **Parked** | On hold | Waiting for internal resolution |

### Approval Permissions Quick Check

| Your Role | Stages You Can Approve |
|-----------|----------------------|
| **VRM** | A→B, D→E |
| **HOD** | B→C (tag end-user), E→F |
| **Supervisor** | B→C (tag end-user) |
| **CO** | D→E |
| **Executive Approver** | F→L3 |
| **Amni Staff (tagged end-user)** | C→D |
| **Admin** | All stages |
