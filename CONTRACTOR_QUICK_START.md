# Contractor Quick Start Guide

## Welcome to the Amni Contractor Portal!

This guide will help you get started quickly with your contractor registration.

---

## Step 1: Create Your Account (5 minutes)

1. **Find your invitation email** from Amni International
   - Subject: "Invitation to Register with Amni"
   - Contains a unique registration link

2. **Click the registration link** in the email

3. **Fill in your details**:
   - First Name, Last Name
   - Email Address (this becomes your username)
   - Phone Number
   - Create a strong password (min 8 characters, uppercase, lowercase, numbers)

4. **Accept terms** and click "Create Account"

**Tip**: Save your password securely. You'll need it to log in.

---

## Step 2: Log In to Your Portal

1. Go to the contractor login page
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click "Login"

**Bookmark this page** for easy access!

---

## Step 3: Complete Your Registration Form

### Understanding the Form

Your registration form is divided into multiple pages and sections:

- **Progress bar** at top shows how much you've completed
- **Required fields** are marked with a red asterisk (*)
- **Help icons (?)** provide guidance for each field

### Filling Out the Form

1. **Work through each section**:
   - Start from the top
   - Fill in all required fields
   - Click on section headers to expand/collapse

2. **Upload documents**:
   - Click "Choose File" or upload button
   - Select your document
   - Wait for upload to complete
   - **Accepted formats**: PDF, JPG, PNG (check each field for specifics)
   - **Max file size**: Usually 5MB per file

3. **Handle certificates** (documents with expiry dates):
   - Upload the certificate file
   - Enter the expiry date
   - System will track expiry and notify you

4. **Save your progress**:
   - Click "Save Draft" to save at any time
   - Form auto-saves periodically
   - You can log out and continue later

5. **Navigate between pages**:
   - Click "Next" to move forward
   - Click "Previous" to go back
   - Review all pages before submitting

### Common Document Requirements

Typically, you'll need to upload:
- Business registration certificate
- Tax identification documents
- Insurance certificates
- Professional licenses
- Bank account information
- Company profile/overview
- References (if applicable)

**Tip**: Prepare these documents before starting the form to save time!

---

## Step 4: Submit Your Application

1. **Review everything**:
   - Go back through all pages
   - Check all information is accurate
   - Verify all files uploaded correctly

2. **Submit**:
   - Click "Submit Application" on the final page
   - Confirm submission

3. **Confirmation**:
   - You'll see a success message
   - Receive confirmation email

**Important**: Once submitted, you cannot edit unless staff returns it to you.

---

## Step 5: Track Your Application Status

### From Your Dashboard

1. Log in to your portal
2. View your dashboard
3. See application status:
   - **Pending**: Under review by staff
   - **In Progress**: Being processed through approval stages
   - **Returned**: Staff requested revisions (action needed!)
   - **Approved**: Successfully approved

### If Staff Returns Your Application

1. You'll receive an email notification
2. Log in to your portal
3. Click "View Application" or "Edit Application"
4. Read staff comments carefully - they'll tell you exactly what to fix
5. Make the requested changes
6. Re-submit

**Tip**: Address ALL staff comments before re-submitting to avoid delays.

---

## Managing Your Dashboard

### Certificate Expiry Tracking

Your dashboard shows:
- **Expiring Soon** (yellow): Certificates expiring within 30 days
- **Expired** (red): Certificates past expiry date
- **Valid** (green): All other certificates

### Renewing Certificates

1. Click "Renew Certificate" on dashboard alert
2. Upload updated certificate
3. Enter new expiry date
4. Click "Update"

**Important**: Keep certificates current to maintain your contractor status!

## Troubleshooting

### Can't log in?
- Check email spelling
- Verify password is correct (case-sensitive)
- Use "Forgot Password" if needed
- Try a different browser
- Clear browser cache

### File won't upload?
- Check file format (PDF, JPG, PNG typically accepted)
- Verify file size under 5MB
- Try compressing large files
- Check internet connection
- Try different browser

### Form won't submit?
- Scroll through all pages to find incomplete required fields
- Look for red asterisks (*) or error messages
- Ensure all file uploads completed
- Verify certificate dates are filled in
- Try "Save Draft" first, then "Submit"

### Application taking too long?
- Check your dashboard for status updates
- Review messages for staff communications
- Verify your contact email is correct
- If urgent, contact Amni staff via messaging system

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I save and continue later?**
A: Yes! Click "Save Draft" and your progress is saved. Log back in anytime to continue.

**Q: Can I edit after submitting?**
A: No, but if changes are needed, staff will return it to you with instructions.

**Q: What if my invitation link expired?**
A: Contact Amni staff to request a new invitation.

**Q: How many documents can I upload?**
A: Varies by field. Most fields allow 1-5 files. Check the field description.

**Q: What if I don't have a required document?**
A: Contact staff via messaging to explain your situation. They may provide alternatives.

**Q: Can I register multiple companies?**
A: Yes, you can submit multiple registrations from the same account.

**Q: Will I be notified of status changes?**
A: Yes, you'll receive email notifications at key stages.
